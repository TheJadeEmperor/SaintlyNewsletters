Here are the best resources for coding practice:

HackerRank is a website that offers programming challenges and competitions to help improve coding skills and connect with other programmers. It is commonly used for job interviews and practicing coding skills.

LeetCode is an online platform that offers coding challenges and interview preparation for software developers. It helps developers improve their problem-solving skills and technical knowledge.

AlgoExpert is a platform that offers curated coding interview questions with video explanations and solutions for software engineers to practice and improve their problem-solving skills.

CodeChef is a competitive programming platform that hosts online contests and challenges. It aims to foster a community of programmers and promote computer science education.
