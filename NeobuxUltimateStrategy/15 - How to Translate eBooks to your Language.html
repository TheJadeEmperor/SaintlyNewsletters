We do understand that not everyone speaks or reads English. That's why we are giving you instructions on how to translate our ebooks into a language of your choice. All you have to do is go to Google Translate.

https://translate.google.com/

For detect language, select English. In the new language area, pick your language. Then click on Documents and search for the ebook on your computer.

Google does all the work for you, it's that easy. Now all you have to do is read the ebook :)
